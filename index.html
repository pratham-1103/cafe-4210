✦ Our Story

A home away
from home

Cafe 4210 was born from a simple dream: to create a warm, welcoming space where every person who walks through the door feels like family. Nestled in the heart of Leonia, NJ, we're a women-owned neighborhood gem where community comes first.

Every cup we craft, every croffle we press — it's done with genuine care and intention. We source our beans locally, rotate our seasonal specials with love, and our staff? They remember your name by your second visit.

The space itself is a living gallery — rotating art, soft fairy lights, cozy nooks and a little garden out back where the sunlight does something magical in the afternoon.
